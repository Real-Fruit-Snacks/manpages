    KTHIEVES(6) Games Manual KTHIEVES(6)

    NAME

    kthieves - X window forty thieves solitaire

    SYNOPSIS

    kthieves

    DESCRIPTION

    Kthieves brings up a window for a forty thieves solatiaire game. Clicking on a card moves it to the "best" legal play. Dragging a card places it (if legal) in the target stack.

    RULES

    Tableaus build down in suit. Foundations build up in suit. You may move one card at a time from the discard to a tableau, from the tableau to a foundation or between tableaus.

    Deal one card onto the discard pile by clicking the deck.

    Goal: move all cards to the foundations.

    AUTHOR

    Keith Packard
